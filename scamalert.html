Scam Alert: Stay Alert, Stay Safe

Scams in Singapore are rising rapidly, with criminals using increasingly sophisticated tactics across messaging apps, social media, and online platforms. Staying alert is the first and most important defence. Always verify unexpected messages, requests for money, or offers that sound too good to be true. Avoid clicking on unfamiliar links, protect your personal information, and take a moment to check before you act. By staying vigilant and informed, we can safeguard ourselves, our families, and our hard-earned money from falling victim to scams.
