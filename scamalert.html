Scam Alert: Stay Alert, Stay Safe

Scams in Singapore are rising rapidly, with criminals using increasingly sophisticated tactics across messaging apps, social media, and online platforms.

Your First Line of Defense

  • Verify unexpected messages and requests for money
  • Avoid clicking on unfamiliar links
  • Protect your personal information
  • Take a moment to check before you act

2025 Scam Statistics

In the first half of 2025, scam cases in Singapore decreased by 26% to 19,665. However, total losses are still high at $456.4 million. Popular platforms for scams include Facebook, WhatsApp, Instagram, TikTok, Telegram, and Carousell. Most victims remain below age 50, with a rising impact on the elderly.
